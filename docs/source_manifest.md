# Source manifest

Synthesizable Milestone 7 cluster sources are `rtl/core/imported/` (the reviewed Sparrow-V scalar closure with local LR/SC decode and DMEM atomic metadata), `rtl/cache/l1_instruction_cache.sv`, `rtl/cache/l1_data_cache.sv`, `rtl/interconnect/` including `coherence_pkg.sv` and `snoopy_coherence_transport.sv`, `rtl/memory/`, and `rtl/top/`. The import closure and upstream paths are documented in [reuse plan](reuse_plan.md); its recorded source revision is `995ea0f9cada63688c9e21e739bd41d6b1c118af`.

`tb/unit/tb_l1i.sv`, `tb/unit/tb_l1d.sv`, `tb/unit/tb_snoopy_transport.sv`, `tb/unit/tb_lrsc_decode.sv`, `tb/coherence/tb_msi_coherence.sv`, `tb/coherence/tb_coherence_random.sv`, `tb/coherence/tb_lrsc_coherence.sv`, and `tb/coherence/tb_atomic_random.sv` are focused tests. `tb/system/tb_cluster.sv`, `tb/system/tb_m1_multicore.sv`, and `tb/system/tb_runtime_workload.sv` exercise four real cores.

`scripts/build_runtime_sw.py` generates the Milestone 7 RV32I/LRSC runtime and workload images. `sw/runtime/`, `sw/linker/sparrow_cluster.ld`, and `sw/workloads/` document the intended software ABI and workload set. Generated images and listings live under ignored `build/sw/`, and local runner summaries live under ignored `reports/`. `scripts/import_sparrow_v.sh` is deterministic synchronization support. No generated simulator files, external repositories, or SparrowML artifacts are tracked.
