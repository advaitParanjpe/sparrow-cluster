| Workload ID | Name | Active harts | Expected result |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | runtime smoke | 1, 2, 4 | last active hart result |
| 2 | atomic counter | 1, 2, 4 | `active * 8` |
| 3 | spinlock | 4 | 24 protected increments |
| 4 | reusable barrier | 1, 2, 4 | `active * 5` |
| 5 | producer-consumer | 2 | 6 payload transfers |
| 6 | shared reduction | 1, 2, 4 | 36 |
| 7 | ownership ping-pong | 2 | 12 transfers |
| 8 | false sharing | 4 | 24 updates |
| 9 | padded sharing comparison | 4 | 24 updates |
| 10 | read mostly | 1, 2, 4 | `active * 784` |
| 11 | mixed private/shared | 1, 2, 4 | `active * 4` |

Control/status words stay in uncached apertures; workload data starts at `0x1000` so normal shared data exercises the coherent L1D path.
